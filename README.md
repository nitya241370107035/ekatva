# Ekatva (एकत्व)

For generations, India’s weavers have worked in isolation, their looms silent without the backing of collective strength. Ekatva changes that.

It turns scattered cooperatives into a unified digital enterprise — where every thread of raw material, every hour of labour, and every rupee of fair wage is recorded with dignity and transparency. Weavers speak to their phones in their own language to update a job. Cooperatives pool their looms to bid for contracts they could never win alone. And every finished piece carries a story that proves the hand behind it was paid what they deserve.

Ekatva is not just software. It is the digital memory and voice of the handloom community — built to ensure that the craft survives, and the craftspeople thrive.
